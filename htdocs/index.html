Delivery of pbf tiles as static hosting

You have "htdocs" folder for static hosting.
If your vector tiles (pbf) are stored in "htdocs" directory, your VT can be accessible at:

http://localhost:8836/(directory name in "htdocs")/{z}/{x}/{y}.pbf

We have sample tile from NaturalEarth. Try pbf_tile/ne-test/1/1/1.pbf

Maps
